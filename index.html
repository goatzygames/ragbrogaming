About Me

Hello, I'm Rag!

I'm a 29-year-old gamer from Sweden, living with my wonderful wife. Gaming has always been a huge part of my life, but over the years, I've expanded my passions into game development.

Currently, I'm working on a very exciting project: An RPG set in the world of Avatar: The Last Airbender, using Unreal Engine. This project, called The Jasmin Dragon, aims to bring fans of the series an immersive adventure.

You can follow my journey as a developer on my YouTube channel and my Discord where I regularly post devlogs and updates about the game. Join me as I bring this project to life!
