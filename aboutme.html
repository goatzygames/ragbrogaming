About Myself

I’ve been developing games with Unreal Engine 5 for two years, but I recently decided to explore web development. Over time, I realized I was getting a bit tired of game development and found myself enjoying web development more. Currently, I’m teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Although I’m learning on my own and not in a formal program, I’m progressing quickly.

Alongside my self-study, I’m also balancing schoolwork, making structured resources like courses, tutorials, and guides invaluable for my learning.

My Current Project

My current project is building this website! My main focus is improving the user experience and continuously working to enhance it. I also take on new projects daily, which helps me strengthen my knowledge of JavaScript, as I already have a foundation in HTML and CSS. Additionally, all my projects are free to copy and use in your own work.

JavaScript is the first programming language I’m committed to fully mastering. With Unreal Engine 5, I didn’t use C++, relying instead on its visual scripting system. This website serves as an excellent opportunity to practice and deepen my understanding of JavaScript!
