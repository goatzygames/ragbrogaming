What is "The Jasmin Dragon"?!

What is "The Jasmin Dragon"? The Jasmin Dragon is a fan made game for those whom wanted to be a bender. Explore the possibilities as any bender you see fit, when you 100% the game campaign as one nation, you can move over to the next. After completing all campaigns, you'll get an achievement.
